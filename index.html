THE FARMER'S APP

AGROWORKS

About the App


The main aim of the app is to provide an app-based solution that can help farmers at the time of farming and also enables them to get maximum profit from their crop. First of its kind and easy to use app exclusively for the farmers that provide guidance on farm management and tips on better production practices in their local language. The app includes an online forum with a simple and friendly interface where they can post queries, seek clarifications and interact with peers and experts. The app also includes an embedded map which shows local farmers and their produce.

App Workflow

The app is basically implemented and focused at 3 levels as proposed in the prototype:-
  • The Crop is listed on Map with contact details
  • Chatbot in place of customer service for handling a small problem
  • Prediction of crop yield and total revenue to be generated from the farm based on previous few years details.
